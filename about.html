Supero is not a firm of practitioners. We are two people who have built, deployed, and lived inside revenue systems. The diagnostic is ours. The accountability is ours.

Why we built this

The diagnostic-first model exists because everything else fails in the same way.

Most revenue interventions start with a solution. A sales methodology. An AI platform. A coaching programme. The solution is deployed before anyone has measured the actual problem. Behaviours change briefly. Numbers move. Then they revert. The system reasserts itself.

Supero starts with measurement. The Conversation Score, Pipeline Integrity Score, AI Maturity Index, and Financial Impact Model are deployed first. We find out where value is leaking, what it costs, and what the recovery is worth before a single recommendation is made.

Our outcomes are linked to agreed KPIs. We have no tools to sell, no methodology to licence, and no incentive to recommend anything other than what the data shows.
